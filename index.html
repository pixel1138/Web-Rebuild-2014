Why Use Us?

Medical Billing & Management Services can save you the hassle, headache, and heartache of dealing with many of the costs that are associated with a billing environment for many different practice disciplines. Many of which are hidden and sometimes rarely thought of until the bills come. We can save you time and money on all of these costs.

Direct Costs of Billing Departments

  • Computer Hardware & Maintenance
  • Computer Software & Upgrades
  • Paper Supplies, Forms, Envelopes & Postage
  • Extra Telephone Systems & Telephone Lines
  • Office Equipment & Furniture for a Billing Staff
  • Wages & Benefits for a Billing Staff

Indirect Costs of Billing Departments

  • Workers' Compensation Fees
  • Unemployment Compensation Fees
  • Expensive Commercial Space for Extra Employees
  • Hiring, Training, & Managing Billing Personnel
  • Accounting Costs for Quarterly/Yearly Reporting
  • Accounting Costs for Extra Employee Bookkeeping
  • Continuing Education for Billing Department
